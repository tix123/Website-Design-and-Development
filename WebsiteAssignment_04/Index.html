1. Introduction

We are going to talk about the effect that we can have of Tesla with the use of our marketing strategies such as lead generation, website enhancement, media strategies, and search engine optimization. We will go further in-depth on these topics in the rest of our report and how we will use these strategies to maximize Tesla’s profits soon.

1.1. Purpose

The purpose of this report is to respond to the Request for Proposal (RFP) issued by Tesla. This RFP is looking for a web design company to build a website with specific demands such as attracting social media followers and lead generation. This report will demonstrate the technical abilities of Petra and respond to these demands in seeking the opportunity to cooperate with Tesla.

1.2. Background

Tesla is one of the leading technology companies in the world. They are looking for an IT company to support their website design for marketing the Tesla electric car. Our company - Petra who has twelve years’ experience and experience working with huge corporations such as Amazon, Facebook, Samsung, etc. We believe the cooperation with Tesla will be a win-win situation.

The scope of this response will cover the following points:

  • Lead Generation:

    • The most significant aspect about lead generation is to attract strangers to become a customer in too many ways of advertising via all social media and channels.

  • Media Strategies:
